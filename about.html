Our Story

A young company with a clear purpose

Dylan Enterprises was founded in 2024 in Lagos, Nigeria, with a simple goal: make it easier for Nigerians to trade with the world and travel it with confidence. What began as a small consultancy handling export documentation and travel bookings has grown into a full-service partner for export, import, shipping & logistics, and faith-based and leisure travel.

Operating from Sultan Plaza, opposite Hanan Store, our team combines hands-on logistics experience with a genuine commitment to hospitality — treating every shipment and every traveller as a personal responsibility.

Our Mission

To provide seamless, reliable and affordable export, import, logistics and travel services that connect Nigerians to global opportunities — handled with honesty, speed and professionalism at every step.

Our Vision

To become West Africa's most trusted name in international trade facilitation and travel consultancy, recognised for excellence, integrity and the enduring relationships we build with every client.
